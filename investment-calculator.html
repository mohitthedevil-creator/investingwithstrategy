How the Investment Calculator Works

This calculator uses standard compound interest and SIP future value formulas, applied month-by-month for accuracy.

  1. Lumpsum Investment Growth: Your one-time investment grows annually at the expected rate of return (CAGR) over the selected time period.
    Formula used: Future Value = Principal √ó (1 + Rate) ^ Time
  2. SIP Investment with Annual Step-Up: Monthly SIP investments are compounded monthly. If you choose an annual step-up, the SIP amount increases every year by the selected percentage, simulating salary growth or income increase. Each SIP installment compounds for the remaining investment duration, giving a realistic estimate of long-term returns.
  3. Combined Investment Corpus: The calculator then combines lumpsum and SIP investment values to give you the total corpus, along with total invested amount and total wealth created through returns.

To keep calculations transparent, this investment calculator assumes:
