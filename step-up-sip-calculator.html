What Is a Step-Up SIP?

A Step-Up SIP (also known as an increasing SIP) allows you to increase your SIP amount every year by a fixed percentage. This strategy helps your investments grow in line with salary hikes and inflation, making it far more powerful than a fixed SIP over the long term.

For example, if you start a SIP of â‚¹10,000 per month and increase it by 10% every year, your investment amount grows automatically without putting pressure on your current finances.

Step-Up SIP vs Regular SIP

  • Monthly Investment: Regular SIP = fixed; Step-Up SIP = increases yearly
  • Inflation Protection: Regular SIP = low; Step-Up SIP = high
  • Wealth Creation: Regular SIP = moderate; Step-Up SIP = significantly higher
  • Suitable For: Regular SIP = short to medium term; Step-Up SIP = long-term goals (10+ years)

A step-up SIP is especially effective for retirement planning and long-term wealth creation.
